Executive Intelligence

ImpactOS

ImpactOS is a decision operating system for executive teams. It provides structured frameworks for strategic planning, organizational health assessment, departmental design, and execution measurement. Leaders generate analysis within governed schemas while maintaining interpretive authority.

Strategic Framework Engine

Organizational Health Monitor

Track organizational performance against sector benchmarks. The platform surfaces financial health, operational capacity, market positioning, and execution readiness without prescribing corrective action.

Instructional Intelligence

ClassroomOS

ClassroomOS is a strategic intelligence platform for instructional environments. It allows educators across roles — teachers, tutors, substitutes, instructional coaches — to document strategy, log evidence, and maintain operational continuity. The system preserves instructional context across personnel transitions.

Strategy Snapshots

Educators create timestamped records of instructional decisions. Each snapshot captures context, rationale, and intended approach. Teachers document their thinking. Substitutes access prior strategies. Coaches review patterns over time.

Evidence Tracking

Structured logs connect observations to strategies. Educators mark what they see — student engagement, comprehension signals, behavioral patterns — building a temporal record without interpretation.

Role-Based Access

Different users see different interfaces. Teachers create snapshots. Tutors view teacher strategies and add their own. Substitutes inherit context. Coaches provide feedback across all documented strategies.

Continuity Across Transitions

When personnel change, the platform preserves strategic context. New educators access prior snapshots, understand existing approaches, and contribute their own observations within the same structural framework.
